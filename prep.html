Career Preparation


Career Preparation may differ slightly based on specializations such as Front-end roles or Back-end roles. This is a general guide:

  • Key Concepts
    • Algorithms
    • Data Structures
    • Big O (Space and Time Complexity)

  • Popular Technologies/Coding Languages
    • Front-end: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, JQuery, Bootstrap, etc.
    • Back-end: Java, Python, SQL, C#, C++, etc.
    • Frameworks: tools that help professionals build apps, websites and digital systems.
      • Ruby on Rails
      • Django
      • React
      • NodeJS
      • Angular

The Interview

Software Engineering positions often require both technical & non-technical interviews. Technical interviews often require a series of rounds that include implementation of a solution to a coding problem in a short period of time. Your performance is measured by your solution and explanations. Other formats of the technical interview include take-home projects or quizzes. It is best to prepare by studying key concepts and problem solving.
