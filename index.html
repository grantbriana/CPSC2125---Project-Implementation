Why Become a Software Engineer?

Software Engineers are found in literally every industry from Government to Fashion. Software Engineers are in high demand, flexible, challenging, suitable for individual work & collaboration, creative outlets and are high-paying. This career choice has a significant impact on technology, communication, education, and more within our society.

Roles & Responsibilities

The role of a software engineer is "to analyze problems & design scalable solutions using software engineering principles. They are also responsible for data designing, acquiring, maintenance and security, test case designing and documentation (Forbes Advisor)."

Types of Software Engineers

  • Front-End Engineer
  • Back-End Engineer
  • Full-Stack Engineer
  • Quality Assurance(QA) Engineer
  • DevOps Engineer
  • Security Engineer
  • Game Designer
  • Mobile Developer
  • Data Scientist
  • Site Reliability Engineer
